Princess Nautilus



Princess Nautilus is the princess of the Ripple Kingdom and the daughter of King Kelp and Queen Conch, providing a source of endless optimism and cheerfulness for the team. While she doesn't directly participate in battles, she's able to use her Royal Instrument of Summoning to summon Liquus, an eel-like creature which is able to shock anything that touches it.
